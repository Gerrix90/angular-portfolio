Get African Business Online

Overview

Get African business online (GAFBO) is a Hostgator.in program run in association with Google. This program helps african business setup their online businesses easily and at a low cost. The Get Africa online and its subpages where build on the in house CMS, Supersite (?) . This project required customizations to tailor the site to a minimalistic purchase flow. Thus some or most of the code on this page maybe considered 'hacky', as the regular flow provided by Supersite had to be altered.
